Every E2B Sandbox ships with an isolated filesystem for creating, reading, writing, watching, and deleting files. The [Hobby tier](https://e2b.dev/pricing) sandboxes come with 10 GB of the free disk space and [Pro tier](https://e2b.dev/pricing) sandboxes come with 20 GB.

With E2B SDK you can:
- [Read and write files to the sandbox.](/docs/filesystem/read-write)
- [Watch directory for changes.](/docs/filesystem/watch)
- [Upload data to the sandbox.](/docs/filesystem/upload)
- [Download data from the sandbox.](/docs/filesystem/download)
